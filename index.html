ylwflwrperilmvmnt

ylwflwrperilmvmnt is a independent media resource organisation that provides a web-based resource response to less social media, more social justice for the youth and adults seeking to connect to the greater minneapolis-st.paul communities.

our content, workshops, allied groups, and organisation protest settler colonialism, partirachy, captialism, and white supremacy aka "whiteness". we strive to instigate meaningful discourse that leads off the page and catalyses political action.

we seek to raise the level and depth of discourse happening in our communities with youth and adults. we prioritize youth, black, indigenous, people of color (b-i-poc), womxn, mxn, and lgbtq+ in order to highlight the complexity of our communities.

core values of ylwflwrperilmvmnt
empathy, non-judgmental, humility, compassion, self-awareness, ability to operate in the grey, commitment to the collective, self-motivated, open and willing to learn/unlearn, ability to build authentic relationships, open and honest communication, passionate, mission alignment

join our mission to change how we connect away from our daily screens:
  • less social media, more social justice.
